The diverse communties in kenya and their cultural foods.

Maasai culture

1

The Maasai community is recognised all over the country for having the strongest culural practices that are still implemented despite the fact that most of the other communities adopt some of the western culture life styles. This is most specifically on their dressing cord and the preservation of their cultural food. All these have made the Maasai community to be outstanding against the other cultures that are present in Kenya.
Their cultural foods are : milk, meat, fat, blood, honey, and tree bark.
These foods are important into the body since they provide the body with important nutrients and minerals like ions, calcium and phosphorous.

Kikuyu culture

2

In kikuyu community is the largest community in Kenya that has been to cover the largest population of the country. Moreover, they occupy the central region in the country that is reach in fertile soils and adiquate rainfall.
Their favourite foods are :Githeri (maize and beans), Mukimo (mashed green peas and potatoes), Kimitu (mashed beans and potatoes), Irio (mashed dry beans, corn and potatoes), Mutura (sausage made using goat intestines, meat and blood), and Ucuru (fermented porridge made from flour of corn.

Luo culture

3

The Luo community is well known in Kenya for the value it has on fish as their staple food. As a culture it has strive towards living maintaining and preserving its traditions.
The traditional foods of this community : Fish, Ugali, Fillet , Omena,  Pumkin leaves, Mashroom, Meat.
Since fish are the main staple food of this community. They offer a very important oil into the body cord liver oil. It help in development of brain cell in children. It also helps in proper functioning of the brain in adults and other developed humans. Furthermore this kind of oil is adversable to be consumed by any one because it is natural it has no effects to the body.

Mijikenda culture

4

This language group is known in Kenya for its testy foods and mostly found in the coastal region of the country as a whole. It is composed of 9 tribes known as Giriama, Digo , Duruma, Chonyi , Kambe , Jibana, Kauma, Rabai and Ribe. All these tribes are linked together as one because most of their cultural practices are similar and they occupy the same region and geographical area , moreover they speak similar and common language that all the members are able to easily communicate to each other.Their favourite cultural foods are:millet, maize, cassava, peas and cotton, with coconut and cassava,Mnavu,
Mzungi and Mchicha.These traditional foods are important in the body beacuse they not only offer significant mineral salts but also they protect and enhance the immunity of the body in terms of fighting diseases causing germs.
